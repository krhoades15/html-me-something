The Boiz®


Hello and welcome to The Boiz® Information Page. Below you will find info about the two Boiz®, Jasper and Ace, that reside with Katy Rhoades. Please enjoy reading about my Boiz® as much as I enjoy talking about them.

Boi 1 - Jasper Wallace


This fine thicc specimen is Jasper Wallace. His birthday is January 2, 2015. My husband Justin and I adopted him from an animal hospital my friend worked at at the time. He was only 5 months old, and the sweetest most snuggly cat I ever met. I fell in love immediately. I was never really a cat person until I took this guy home. Life was never the same, and it continues to get better every day.

Fun facts about Jasper:

  • Also goes by: Tubbs McGubbs, Thiccums, Jasper ShitWallace
  • Favorite activities include sleeping and begging to be fed
  • Weighs 2 lbs more than Ace
  • Loves me more than Justin and makes it obvious
  • Is the most tolerant cat in the world

Boi 2 - Ace Neo


Here we have the last but not least boi, Ace Neo. his birthday is February 5, 2017. Ace was adopted on kind of a whim. Justin and I have always talked about getting a dog, but our work schedules were wonky and dogs are a lot of responsibility. However, we made the plunge one day and adopted this little prince and our family became complete.

Fun facts about Ace:

  • Also goes by: Acerino, Aceroni and Cheese, Puppers
  • Favorite activities include walkies, nappies, zoomies, and tearing things up he's not supposed to
  • Has had surgery to remove a bread tie from his intestines (because he's a sneaky dummy and ate one)
  • His breed is piebald dachshund
  • Has a bad habit of peeing all over when he's excited
